# What is FlowGPT?

FlowGPT is a community-driven platform that allows users to share and discover AI prompts. AI prompts are instructions that can be given to AI models, such as ChatGPT, to generate text, translate languages, write different kinds of creative content, and answer your questions in an informative way.

FlowGPT has a number of features that make it a valuable tool for anyone who wants to use AI models to their full potential. These features include:

- A large and growing library of AI prompts
- The ability to create and share your own AI prompts
- A community of users who are passionate about AI
- A user-friendly interface

If you are interested in using AI models to generate text, translate languages, write different kinds of creative content, or answer your questions in an informative way, then FlowGPT is a great place to start.

## How does FlowGPT work?

FlowGPT works by allowing users to share and discover AI prompts. AI prompts are instructions that can be given to AI models, such as ChatGPT, to generate text, translate languages, write different kinds of creative content, and answer your questions in an informative way.

When a user shares an AI prompt on FlowGPT, it is added to the platform's library of prompts. Other users can then find and use this prompt to generate text, translate languages, write different kinds of creative content, or answer their questions.

Users can also create their own AI prompts. To do this, they simply need to enter the instructions they want to give to the AI model in the prompt creation form. Once the prompt is created, it can be shared with other users on FlowGPT.
